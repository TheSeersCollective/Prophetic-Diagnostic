A Prophetic Diagnostic

What type of prophet are you?

Scripture names eight distinct prophetic functions, each with its own Hebrew root, grammar, and ministry grace. You carry at least one of them.

Most mature prophets carry more than one. Samuel was both Roeh and Nabi. Gad was both Chozeh and Nabi. Ezekiel was a Tsaphah who saw in Chozeh-visions. Habakkuk opens his book as a Nabi carrying a Massa received through Chozeh-vision. Hybridity is the biblical norm.

This diagnostic walks you through sixteen questions, grounded in the Word, to reveal your prophetic signature — whether that is a single dominant grace or one of the named biblical hybrids.
